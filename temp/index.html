About Me

I am currently looking for a UX or Web developer job, through Oregon State University I have obtained my Bachelors of Science in Computer Science with an Applied option in Human-Computer Interaction. The idea of working for a new company sounds like an intriguing new experience where I can utilize all of the skills that I have obtained during my time in college and as a student developer.

Over a 9 month period, I lead a team of two other students though a successful development of a mobile application. I also worked with a team at the Center for Applied Systems and Software to develop new features for a few Oregon State government projects. Stemming from these opportunities, I have gained a concrete understanding of how to successfully complete projects from start to finish. During group projects I have learned that teamwork is vital in the development process of creating and implementing new applications.
